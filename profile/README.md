Welcome to Capital Robotics!

We are Capital Robotics, an enthusiastic and passionate team participating in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC). Our mission is to inspire young minds through science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) by building innovative robots and competing in exciting challenges.

## At Capital Robotics, we focus on:

- Designing and building competitive robots for FTC challenges.
- Collaborating as a team to solve complex problems.
- Learning and applying real-world engineering and coding skills.
- Promoting STEM education in our community.

We are always looking for ways to grow and improve, whether it’s through collaboration with other teams, gaining support from sponsors, or sharing our knowledge with the broader robotics community. If you’re interested in partnering with us or learning more, feel free to get in touch!
